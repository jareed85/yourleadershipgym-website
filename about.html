About Leadership Gym

Your daily workout for leadership.

We believe leadership is a skill you grow through consistent practice—not theory. Just like a gym builds physical strength, Leadership Gym builds your leadership muscles through daily reps.

Every day, you get a real-world leadership prompt. You write your response. AI gives you instant feedback with scores across four dimensions:

  • Clarity — Can you articulate a clear vision or decision?
  • Strategy — Do you think ahead and see the bigger picture?
  • Empathy — Do you understand and connect with your team?
  • Self-Awareness — Do you know your blind spots and growth areas?

Over time, you build a track record. Weekly recap emails summarize your progress. The dashboard shows your trends. You're not just hoping to get better—you're measuring it.
