Frequently Asked Questions

What is Leadership Gym?

Leadership Gym is your space to build stronger leadership habits—one daily rep at a time. You get a daily AI-powered prompt, instant scoring across Clarity, Strategy, Empathy, and Self-Awareness, plus progress charts and weekly recaps.

Who is Leadership Gym for?

Managers, founders, team leads, and anyone who wants to improve their leadership skills through daily reflection and AI feedback.

How does the free trial work?

Enjoy a 7-day free trial—no credit card required. You’ll get full access to all features. At the end of 7 days, you’ll be prompted to subscribe to continue.

How much time does it take daily?

The average session takes about 5 minutes, making it easy to build into your morning routine.

How do I view my past reflections and scores?

Open the app and select “History” from the menu to see all your past prompts and scores over time.

What if I need to cancel?

Subscriptions are handled through Stripe; you can cancel anytime via your Stripe customer portal. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes—your reflections and scores are stored securely and only you can access them. We do not share your personal data with third parties.

How can I get help or support?

Email us at support@leadershipgymhq.com and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.
