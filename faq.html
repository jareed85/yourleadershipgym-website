Frequently Asked Questions

What is Leadership Gym?

Leadership Gym is your space to build stronger leadership habits — one daily rep at a time. You get a real workplace scenario every day, write how you'd handle it, and receive instant AI scores across Clarity, Strategy, Empathy, and Self-Awareness.

Who is it for?

Managers, founders, team leads, and anyone who wants to sharpen their leadership instincts through daily practice and honest feedback.

Is it really free?

Yes — the core experience is free forever. You get unlimited daily scenarios, unlimited submissions, and your scores across all 4 dimensions at no cost. Upgrading to Pro ($2.99/mo) unlocks full AI coaching feedback and a personalised focus area after each session.

How much time does it take?

About 5 minutes a day. Read the scenario, write your response, get your scores. That's it.

How do I see my past reflections?

Open the app and tap the History tab to see all your past responses and scores over time.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Sign in to the app at leadershipgymhq.com, submit today's reflection, and tap Upgrade Now in the feedback section. It's $2.99/mo — cancel anytime.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are handled through Stripe. You can cancel anytime via your Stripe customer portal — your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your reflections and scores are stored securely and only accessible to you. We never share your personal data with third parties.

How do I get support?

Email us at support@leadershipgymhq.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
