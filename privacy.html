Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2025

Information We Collect

When you sign up for Leadership Gym (built on Glide), we collect:

  • Your name (as entered in the sign-up form)
  • Your email address (to create your account and send weekly recaps)

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and personalize your Leadership Gym experience
  • To send you weekly summary emails of your progress and next-step tips
  • To respond to support inquiries at support@leadershipgymhq.com

Third-Party Services

Leadership Gym is powered by Glide and hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Your data is stored on Glide’s secure servers. We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Update your name or email in the app at any time
  • Unsubscribe from recap emails via the link in each email
  • Delete your account by emailing support@leadershipgymhq.com

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at support@leadershipgymhq.com.
